# gamezone-sales-performance-overview-and-trend-breakdowns-English-
This project explores sales transaction data to understand revenue trends, customer purchasing patterns, and product performance. The analysis was conducted in Excel and presented through an interactive dashboard highlighting key metrics such as revenue, orders, and average order value (AOV).
# Gamezone Sales Performance Analysis

## Company Background

Gamezone is a company operating in the gaming equipment industry and has been active since 2010. The company sells gaming products through both online and offline channels.

The dataset used in this project contains information on product prices, orders, marketing channels, countries, and regions. This project aims to evaluate sales performance and support data-driven decisions to improve overall revenue.

Insights and recommendations are provided in the following areas:

1. **Sales Performance** – Analysis of total revenue from 2019–2020, average order value, and order volume  
2. **Product Performance** – Evaluation of product-level sales to identify top-performing and underperforming products  
3. **Customer Behavior** – Identification of high-season periods based on purchasing patterns  
4. **Regional Analysis** – Analysis of sales performance by country to identify top contributing markets

# Executive Summary

## Overview of Findings

Total revenue from 2019 to 2021 reached approximately **$6.1M**. However, the dataset for 2021 only includes **January–February**, therefore the Year-over-Year comparison focuses on **2019–2020** to provide a more relevant analysis.

Overall, the company experienced significant growth from **2019 to 2020**:

- **Revenue increased by 164% YoY**
- **Orders increased by 102% YoY**
- **Average Order Value (AOV) increased by 30% YoY**

This growth suggests a strong surge in demand during 2020, potentially influenced by changes in consumer behavior during the pandemic period.

---

# Key Insights

## 1. Sales Performance

- Sales peaked in **December 2020** with total revenue of **$549,435.13**, representing a significant spike compared to other months within the same year. This indicates strong seasonal demand during year-end purchasing periods.
- Order volume also increased substantially from **2019 to 2020**, aligning with the overall revenue growth trend.
- Since the dataset for **2021 only includes January–February**, it is difficult to determine whether the upward trend continued throughout the year.
- Interestingly, the **AOV for early 2021 ($291.39)** is higher than the **full-year AOV in 2019 ($233.06)**. However, because the 2021 dataset only covers January–February, this observation should be interpreted cautiously. It may indicate a potential increase in spending per transaction, but further data is required to confirm this trend.

---

## 2. Product Performance

- **96% of total revenue** is generated by four products:
  - 27in 4K Gaming Monitor  
  - Lenovo Ideapad Gaming 3  
  - Nintendo Switch  
  - Sony PlayStation 5 Bundle  

  Together, these products contributed approximately **$5.95M** in revenue.

- The **27in 4K Gaming Monitor** generated the highest revenue between **2019–2020**, reaching around **$1.9M**.
- In contrast, the **Razer Pro Gaming Headset** generated the lowest revenue, contributing only around **$800**.
- **88% of total order volume** comes from three products:
  - 27in 4K Gaming Monitor  
  - Lenovo Ideapad Gaming 3  
  - Nintendo Switch  

- The **Nintendo Switch** recorded the highest order volume, accounting for **47% of total orders**, while the **Razer Pro Gaming Headset** had the lowest order volume at **0.03%**.
- There is **no recorded sales data for the Razer Pro Gaming Headset in 2019**, and only **7 units were sold in 2020**. This product appears to be significantly underperforming, although the issue may also be related to missing or incomplete data. Further clarification from stakeholders may be required.

---

## 3. Customer Behavior

- Sales consistently **peak in December**, likely driven by the holiday season and year-end shopping period.
- The significant increase in sales during **2020** may also be influenced by changes in consumer behavior during the pandemic, as more consumers shifted toward online purchases and home entertainment products.
- Most transactions come from the **direct channel**, suggesting that customers primarily purchase directly rather than through email or social media channels.

---

## 4. Regional Analysis

- **North America (NA)** generated the highest total revenue, contributing approximately **$3.2M**, with the **United States** as the largest contributor at around **$2.9M**.

Top revenue by region:

- **APAC**: Japan (~$220K)  
- **EMEA**: United Kingdom (~$475K)  
- **LATAM**: Brazil (~$146K)  
- **North America**: United States (~$2.9M)

Some transactions contain **missing country codes**, which were categorized as **"Unknown"** in the analysis.

---

# Insights

- Revenue is highly concentrated, with **96% generated from only four products**, indicating strong reliance on a small product group.
- Although **Nintendo Switch accounts for the highest order volume (47%)**, the **27in 4K Gaming Monitor generates the highest revenue**, suggesting a higher average selling price.
- **North America contributes more than half of total revenue**, indicating strong market dependence on this region.

---

# Recommendations

- Since **96% of total revenue is generated by only four products**, the marketing and product teams should evaluate the performance of the remaining products and explore strategies to improve their contribution.
- The company may also consider **diversifying its product revenue mix** to reduce dependence on a small number of products.
- Given that **North America contributes the majority of revenue**, the company could explore opportunities to **expand sales performance in other regions**.
